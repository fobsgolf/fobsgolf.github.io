Handicap

After each round - handicap is adjusted according to score.
Any score larger than handicap, a maximum of 0.2 will be added to handicap.
For every shot you hit under your handicap, 0.2 per shot will be deducted
from your handicap. Eg. if your handicap is 20 and scored 10 over,
10 x 0.2 = 2 strokes will be deducted for next round. 18 will be the new
handicap for next round.
